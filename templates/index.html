About Me

Howdy ! My name is Timothy Baney, and I'm a web developer. I went to school in Arizona, drifting through majors like many others do until finally discovering the amazing world of code, and setting my sites on Computer Science. I received certification from a coding bootcamp, and eventually received a job offer in northwest Arkansas at a company called Mill Creek Software where I create content managed web applications for clients of all sorts. I live with my beautiful fiance, and 4 party animals. When I'm not working on the information super highway, I like kayaking, biking, and trying to tire out my german shepherd. I also love to travel. If you'd like to get in touch with me about a website, remote work, or even just to give me feedback about this site, please email me at timbaney1989@gmail.com
